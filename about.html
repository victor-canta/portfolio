About Me

My journey with data analysis began at The Blackman Laboratory, where I first applied domain knowledge and developed my programming skills. After graduating from UC Berkeley with a B.S. in Genetics and Plant Biology, I sharpened my programming and cloud computing skills as a Bioinformatics Intern at @Sound Agriculture. Most recently, I have continued my journey as a Data Analyst Apprentice with my amazing peers and captains at @COOP Careers.

With the conclusion of my data analytics apprenticeship at the end of this year, I will continue preparing myself with data science tools by pursuing a Master of Science Degree in Data Science this upcoming January 2024.
